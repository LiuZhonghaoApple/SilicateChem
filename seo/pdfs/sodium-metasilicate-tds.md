## 2. Product Description

Sodium metasilicate is an inorganic silicate salt used as an alkaline builder in detergent formulations, industrial cleaning compounds, ceramic processing, and water treatment applications. Shandong Zhongzhi Chemical manufactures sodium metasilicate at our Shandong facility with in-house granulation, drying, and quality control.

**Supply model:** Factory-direct from manufacturer. No trading intermediary.

**Annual capacity:** 100,000+ metric tons.

## 8. Storage and Handling

- Store in a cool, dry, well-ventilated area away from incompatible materials (acids, aluminum, reactive metals).
- Keep containers tightly closed when not in use.
- Protect from moisture uptake; hygroscopic grades require sealed storage.
- Use appropriate PPE: dust mask, safety glasses, gloves. Refer to MSDS for full safety guidance.
